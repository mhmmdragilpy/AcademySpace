1. General Rules

All facilities must be used responsibly. Users are expected to maintain cleanliness and order. Damage to property will result in fines.

2. Booking Procedure

  1. Login with your Telkom University account.
  2. Check availability of the desired facility.
  3. Submit a booking request at least 3 days in advance.
  4. Wait for admin approval via email or dashboard notification.
  5. Print the approval letter (if required) and bring it to the security post.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled time. Failure to cancel in time may affect future booking privileges.

4. Penalties

Users found violating these rules may be banned from using the system for up to one semester.
